# .github

Organization-wide templates for issues, PRs, security policy, and support docs for **Cassilly Capital LLC** and its portfolio companies.

## Corporate Structure

**Cassilly Capital LLC** is the holding company. **STR Capital LLC** is a separate standalone entity (same beneficial owner, NOT a DBA of Cassilly).

Five operating subsidiaries under Cassilly Capital:
- **PES Global Group LLC** (Supply Universe) — owns Portlandia Electric Supply LLC
- **Big Sky Dynamics LLC** (IT/AI Universe) — dba BSDyno
- **Dynamic Distribution Solutions LLC** (Logistics Universe) — dba Portlandia Logistics
- **Portlandia Logistics LLC** (Standalone US 3PL)
- **Villeside Realty LLC** (Real Estate Holding)

> **Canonical Reference**: See [COMPANY_STRUCTURE.md](https://github.com/PESConnect/pes-odoo-erp-infrastructure/blob/main/docs/COMPANY_STRUCTURE.md) for the full 28-entity hierarchy with correct legal names, billing matrix, and Odoo mapping.
